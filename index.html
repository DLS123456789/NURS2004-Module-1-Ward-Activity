What You Learned
Law vs Ethics — Law is what you must do, ethics is what you should do. Often the same, sometimes in tension.
The Dying Patient — Acknowledge the question, support the patient, ensure the right conversation happens.
Good Ethics Needs Good Data — Ethical reasoning fails without accurate facts about the patient and situation.
Two Theory Families — Deontology focuses on duty, teleology focuses on consequences.
Kerridge Model — Six steps: state problem, get facts, consider principles, view perspectives, weigh options, act and evaluate.
